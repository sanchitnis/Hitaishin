# Prabodhini Catalyst OS — Coaching Intake Protocol
## For the Coach: Complete Onboarding Flow for a New Mentee

---

> *"The physician who does not ask questions diagnoses by accident."*
> — Adapted from the Charaka Samhita tradition

---

## OVERVIEW

The intake process is a **3-stage journey** completed over 1–2 weeks:

| Stage | Format | Time Required | Output |
|-------|--------|--------------|--------|
| **Stage 1**: Deep Intake Interview | Guided conversation with coach | 60–90 min | `profiles/[name]-intake.md` |
| **Stage 2**: Psychometric Suite | 3 interactive web apps | 45–60 min total | Results auto-appended to profile |
| **Stage 3**: Synthesis & First Sankalpa | Coach + mentee session | 45 min | `profiles/[name]-profile.md` (living document) |

---

## STAGE 1: THE DEEP INTAKE INTERVIEW

*Conducted by the coach (AI or human). Record responses in `profiles/[name]-intake.md`.*
*Approach: warm, curious, unhurried. This is Svadhyaya — not a form-fill.*

---

### MODULE A: WHO YOU ARE

**A1. Identity Foundations**
- What is your full name, age, and current city?
- Where did you grow up? What was the environment like — family, neighbourhood, values?
- When and how did you connect with Jnana Prabodhini? Which batch/year?
- What is the one thing JP gave you that you carry every single day?
- How do you describe yourself when no title, role, or organization is mentioned?

**A2. The Inheritance**
- What values did your family of origin instil in you that still govern you?
- What values did your family instil that you've consciously *revised or rejected*?
- Who is the most formative person in your life (JP or otherwise) — and what did they teach you?
- What philosophical or spiritual tradition do you actively draw from today?

**A3. Prabodhini Formation**
- Which JP concepts, practices, or teachings do you find yourself returning to most?
  (Probes: Organizational Attitude, Svadhyaya, Gunavikas, SPG, Dasabodha, Advaita...)
- What did JP train in you that you feel you've under-used as an adult?
- Is there a JP teaching you intellectually accepted but haven't yet embodied? What is it?

---

### MODULE B: LIFE AS IT IS

**B1. The Five Spheres — Current Reality**
*(Ask each sphere separately. Let them talk. Don't rush.)*

🔵 **Atma (Inner Life)**
- Describe your current inner life — Sadhana, contemplation, spiritual practice.
- How connected do you feel to your deeper purpose right now? (1–10 and why)
- When did you last feel genuinely at peace — not just distraction-free, but actually still?

🟡 **Sangha (Family & Close Relationships)**
- Describe your primary family situation (spouse/partner, children, parents, siblings).
- What is the quality of your most important relationships right now?
- Where do you feel most seen? Where do you feel most alone?
- What is the relationship that most needs your attention — and what's stopping you?

🟠 **Karma Bhoomi (Professional/Creative)**
- What is your current professional role and how long have you been in it?
- On a good day at work, what are you doing and how does it feel?
- On a bad day, what is happening and what does it cost you?
- Is your current work aligned with your Svadharma, or is it a means to something else?
- What professional achievement are you most proud of — and why?

🟢 **Samaj Seva (National/Community Contribution)**
- Do you currently have an organized contribution to India or community beyond your job?
  (An Adult SPG — a chosen social domain you actively work in)
- If yes: describe it. What are you building, with whom, toward what?
- If no: what would you choose if you committed today? What holds you back?

🔴 **Sharira-Prana (Physical/Vital Energy)**
- How would you honestly describe your physical health and energy levels?
- What is your current practice for exercise, sleep, nutrition?
- When do you feel most alive physically? When do you feel most depleted?

**B2. The Integrity Gap**
- Which of the five spheres is most neglected right now? Why?
- If your closest person described your life to a stranger, what would they say about your priorities?
- What does your actual calendar show vs. what you say matters most to you?

---

### MODULE C: THE VISION

**C1. The 10-Year Picture**
*Ask them to close their eyes and imagine 10 years from now. A morning in their life.*
- Where are they? What does the environment look like?
- What work are they doing, and with whom?
- What has been built — that didn't exist before?
- Who is beside them, and what is the quality of those relationships?
- What does their inner state feel like?

**C2. Svadharma — The Calling**
- If you had unlimited time and resources and could not fail — what would you choose to build for India?
- What problem, if you helped solve it, would make your life feel meaningful regardless of recognition?
- What are you secretly afraid you might be called to do — that you haven't yet answered?

**C3. Sankalpas — Current Vision**
Ask them to articulate (roughly, this will be refined):
- A **1-year Sankalpa**: The primary commitment for this year, across all spheres
- A **5-year vision**: The chapter title for where they want to be in 5 years
- A **SPG commitment**: The contribution they will organize beyond their professional role

---

### MODULE D: PASSIONS, HOBBIES & THE WHOLE PERSON

**D1. Passions and Deep Interests**
- What topics, when you encounter them, make you completely lose track of time?
- What do you read for pleasure — not for professional development, but pure love?
- What art form, craft, or creative practice do you engage with? Even occasionally?

**D2. Hobbies and Recreation**
- What do you do that has no productive purpose — that you do purely for joy?
- What did you love doing at age 12 that you've abandoned? Is there something to reclaim?
- Who do you become when you're engaged in your hobbies? Is that person's energy accessible at other times?

**D3. Flow States**
- Describe the last time you were in genuine Flow — absorbed, effortless, alive.
  What were you doing? What enabled it?
- What are the conditions (environment, time of day, type of work) that most reliably create Flow for you?
- What are the reliable Flow-killers in your current life?

---

### MODULE E: PATTERNS, SHADOW, AND GROWTH EDGES

**E1. Recurring Patterns**
- What is the challenge that keeps appearing in different forms throughout your life?
- What do people who know you well consistently appreciate about you — and what do they find difficult?
- In leadership situations, what is your default strength — and your default shadow?

**E2. The Inner Critic**
- What is the harshest thing your inner voice says about you?
- In what situations does self-doubt most reliably appear?
- What is the standard you hold yourself to — and where did that standard come from?

**E3. Growth Edges**
*(These will be confirmed by psychometric results)*
- What capacity, if significantly developed, would most transform your effectiveness and fulfilment?
- What are you currently avoiding learning or confronting?
- What would a JP teacher who truly knew you say is your primary growth edge right now?

---

### MODULE F: COACHING EXPECTATIONS

**F1. This Coaching Relationship**
- What do you most want from this coaching process?
- What would make this a waste of time for you?
- What does the coach need to know about how to best support you?
- Are you more likely to benefit from challenge or from support? (Or both — be specific)
- What is the one thing you hope this coaching process helps you become?

**F2. Accountability Style**
- How do you prefer to be held accountable? (Direct, gentle, data-based, narrative?)
- What do you want the coach to do when you're rationalizing instead of being honest?
- How frequently do you want to check in? (Daily quick, weekly deep, monthly compass?)

---

## STAGE 2: PSYCHOMETRIC SUITE

*Three interactive web apps — open each in browser. Results saved to profile folder.*

### Test 1: VIA Character Strengths
**File**: `intake/apps/01-character-strengths.html`
**What it measures**: Your top character strengths from the 24 VIA strengths
**JP alignment**: Svabhava (natural disposition) + Gunavikas (which qualities are most developed)
**Time**: 15–20 minutes | 30 questions

### Test 2: Five Spheres Balance Wheel
**File**: `intake/apps/02-spheres-assessment.html`
**What it measures**: Detailed current-state across all Five Spheres + integrity gap analysis
**JP alignment**: Integral Life audit; Paristhiti Jnana of one's own life
**Time**: 10–15 minutes | 50 questions (10 per sphere)

### Test 3: Organizational Attitude & Leadership Style
**File**: `intake/apps/03-leadership-oa-profile.html`
**What it measures**: Strength on each OA component + adaptive vs. technical leadership preference + Saboteur profile
**JP alignment**: Direct Vivekananda OA framework; Sthitaprajna vs. reactive profile
**Time**: 15–20 minutes | 40 questions

---

## STAGE 3: SYNTHESIS SESSION

*After Stages 1 and 2 are complete, the coach conducts a 45-minute synthesis conversation.*

### Coach's Synthesis Protocol:

1. **Read back** the intake responses and psychometric highlights to the mentee (10 min)
   — "Here is what I heard. Here is what the data shows. Here is what stands out."

2. **Identify the Central Theme** — the one thread that runs through all the data (5 min)
   — Not a diagnosis. A pattern. "I notice that across everything you've shared, there is a recurring..."

3. **The Three Growth Edges** — confirm with mentee (10 min)
   — Primary (most urgent), Secondary (most transformative), Long-arc (most meaningful)

4. **Craft the Coaching Sankalpas** — together (10 min)
   — One per sphere; one for the coaching engagement as a whole

5. **Agree the Coaching Cadence** (5 min)
   — Daily/Weekly/Monthly rhythm; check-in format; accountability style

6. **Create `profiles/[name]-profile.md`** — the living document (ongoing)
