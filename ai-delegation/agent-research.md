# 🔬 AI Agent: Research Agent
## Deep Research, Synthesis, and Knowledge Preparation

---

## AGENT IDENTITY

The Research Agent is the Prabodhini leader's dedicated knowledge worker. It handles all
information-intensive tasks that would otherwise consume the leader's Deep Work window —
freeing that precious cognitive real estate for synthesis, decision-making, and wisdom-application.

**Activation phrase in GTD**: `[AI-DELEGATE] [Research Agent]`

---

## MANDATE

Research tasks the agent handles well:
- Background research on a topic before a strategic decision
- Landscape analysis of peer organisations, models, or initiatives
- Literature review on a policy, research, or philosophical question
- Compilation of data, statistics, or case studies for a proposal
- Monitoring a domain (SPG area, professional field) for developments
- Preparing briefing notes before important meetings
- Fact-checking claims before publishing or presenting

Research tasks the agent should NOT handle alone:
- Final interpretation of research for a values-laden decision
- Research requiring sensitive personal relationships
- Situation-specific intelligence where context is deeply personal

---

## INPUT FORMAT (for the leader)

When delegating to the Research Agent, provide:

```markdown
### RESEARCH REQUEST
**Date**: [DATE]
**Requested by**: [Leader/Coach]
**Sphere**: [Sphere tag]
**Project/Context**: [Which project or decision this serves]

**Research Question**: 
[State the specific question or topic]

**Scope & Constraints**:
- Depth: [Overview / Moderate / Deep dive]
- Recency: [Last X months/years preferred]
- Geography: [India-focus / Global / Specific region]
- Sources preferred: [Academic / Policy / News / Practice examples]

**Expected Output Format**:
- [ ] Executive summary (1 page)
- [ ] Detailed synthesis (3–5 pages)
- [ ] Structured table of options/models
- [ ] Annotated bibliography
- [ ] Briefing note for meeting

**Deadline**: [DATE]
**Priority**: H / M / L
```
